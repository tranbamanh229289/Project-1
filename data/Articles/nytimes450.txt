Audrey Marie Christine Dorélien and Jason Theodore Kerwin are to be married June 26 at the Mill City Museum in Minneapolis. The Rev. Stephen L. Prokop, a priest of the American Apostolic Catholic Church, is to officiate.
The bride, 33, and groom, 32, work at the University of Minnesota. She is an assistant professor at the Humphrey School of Public Affairs in Minneapolis, and he is an assistant professor in the applied economics department, in Falcon Heights, Minn.
Ms. Dorélien, who will keep her name, graduated from Swarthmore and received a doctoral degree in public affairs from Princeton. She is a daughter of Dr. Laure Laroche Dorélien and Edwidge Y. Dorélien of Boca Raton, Fla.
Mr. Kerwin graduated from Stanford and received a doctoral degree in economics from the University of Michigan. He is a son of Lynda Bruhl Kerwin and Brad T. Kerwin of Honolulu.
The couple met in 2012, when Mr. Kerwin approached Ms. Dorélien in San Francisco at a conference, and got together a year later through OkCupid.
